 Q1. What problem are you solving?
5
 Suggested Answer Source Tab Founder Review Flag
6
 Operations leaders at multi-location organizations in India - retail chains, facility management companies, hotels, and hospitals - have no way to connect physical touchpoint feedback to real-time service resolution. Feedback is collected through paper forms, generic survey links, or siloed tools. Negative responses are missed, manually routed through WhatsApp, or discovered weeks later in a monthly report. By that time, the service failure has already cost repeat purchases, SLA penalties, or OTA rating points. Survey solves this by linking QR-based location-specific feedback, automatic ticket creation from negative responses, and helpdesk visibility in a single platform without requiring third-party integrations.  Q3. What is your revenue model?
13
 Suggested Answer Source Tab Founder Review Flag
14
 Survey is priced as a SaaS subscription on a per-location per-month basis for India and on a flat monthly tier for global customers. Launch pricing is INR 1,999/location/month for up to 5 locations (annual billing) and INR 4,999/month flat for up to 20 locations. At 6 months post-launch, module-based add-ons will be introduced for WhatsApp distribution and AI sentiment analysis. Enterprise customers with 50+ locations will be priced on a custom annual contract with a data residency premium. Revenue is recurring, annual-billing preferred, with no per-response overage charges at standard tiers.  Q4. What is your competitive advantage?
17
 Suggested Answer Source Tab Founder Review Flag
18
 Survey is the only India-built platform that natively combines QR-to-location mapping with unique-per-mapping codes, automatic ticket creation from negative survey responses, and a ticket detail panel embedded inside the response view showing TAT, escalation level, and resolution status. No India-market competitor - including Zonka Feedback, SurveySensum, and SurveySparrow - offers these three capabilities natively. Zonka requires Zapier or Zendesk connectors for ticket creation. SurveySensum has no physical touchpoint module. HappyOrNot requires proprietary hardware with no resolution workflow. Survey delivers all three in a self-serve cloud platform at Indian pricing.  Q9. What are the key risks and how will you mitigate them?
37
 Suggested Answer Source Tab Founder Review Flag
38
 Risk 1: WhatsApp and AI sentiment gaps cost enterprise deals in months 1 to 6. Mitigation: Phase 1 roadmap prioritizes WhatsApp and AI sentiment for delivery by Month 3. Risk 2: Zonka Feedback or SurveySensum adds native ticket automation. Mitigation: Deepen moat with embedded ticket panel features and data residency option that global competitors cannot easily match. Risk 3: Enterprise procurement cycle is 3 to 6 months, delaying revenue. Mitigation: Target mid-market (INR 2 to 10 Lakh/year contracts) in Year 1 for faster close cycles, build enterprise pipeline in parallel. Risk 4: Response rate fatigue at locations reduces QR scan volume. Mitigation: Enable form mode, WhatsApp push, and multilingual support to increase participation across demographics.
