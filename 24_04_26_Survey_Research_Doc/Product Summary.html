 THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
12
 Core pain point Organizations across retail, hospitality, facilities, campuses, and public services collect feedback through paper forms, basic digital surveys, or fragmented tools that do not connect to operations. Negative responses are missed or manually routed, resulting in delayed resolution and recurring complaints at the same locations.
13
 The data sovereignty gap Most survey platforms are global SaaS tools (SurveyMonkey, Qualtrics, Typeform) that store response data on foreign servers, raise compliance concerns for Indian public sector and regulated enterprise clients, and offer no India-specific support or pricing alignment. Survey is India-built with data residency options suitable for enterprise and government-adjacent clients.
14
 The switching cost trap Competitors lock organizations into proprietary analytics platforms with no QR-to-location mapping, no automatic ticket generation from negative responses, and no helpdesk team routing. Switching means rebuilding survey logic and losing historical response data. Survey offers direct API integrations and Excel exports, keeping data portable and reducing switching cost fears.

 FEATURE SUMMARY (WHAT IS BUILT)
23
 Survey is built around three integrated workflows: (1) Question Setup - admins create survey questions using emoji rating, star rating, multiple choice, or text input, store reusable question banks, configure background branding, enable single-page form mode, and set negative-response triggers that display an additional field prompting the respondent to identify the exact reason for dissatisfaction. (2) Survey Mapping - each survey or question is mapped to a physical location, generating a unique QR code that respondents scan via Google Lens or any QR reader. Locations can be bulk-imported, toggled active or inactive, and viewed in a central mapping list with filter and export. (3) Response Intelligence and Helpdesk - the Survey Info Dashboard shows total responses, per-question distribution, CSAT score, and a heat-map view. Negative responses automatically raise a ticket visible within the response detail view, showing ticket ID, assignment, TAT, priority, escalation status, and resolution timeline. A trend and pattern view enables analytics teams to track sentiment changes over time and identify recurring issues at specific locations.

 WHERE WE ARE TODAY
26
 Product status Core platform is built and functional. Question setup, survey mapping, QR generation, response capture, ticket creation, and dashboard analytics are all operational. Bulk location import, mapping controls, and Excel export are live.
27
 What is missing right now Native integrations with third-party helpdesk platforms (Freshdesk, Zendesk, Zoho Desk). WhatsApp and SMS survey distribution. AI-powered sentiment analysis and automated theme detection on open-text responses. Mobile kiosk app mode for offline response capture.
28
 Competitive moat today No India-built competitor combines QR-to-location mapping with negative-response-to-ticket automation and an embedded ticket visibility panel inside the response detail view. Competitors like Zonka Feedback and SurveySensum require separate helpdesk integrations. Survey does this natively.
29
 Key markets Retail chains (50+ locations), commercial facility management companies, hospitality groups, corporate campuses, healthcare outpatient facilities, public utilities, and educational institutions across Tier 1 and Tier 2 India.
30
 Revenue model SaaS subscription priced per location per month (India) or per organization tier (enterprise annual). Add-on pricing for white-label QR branding, advanced analytics, and custom integrations.
