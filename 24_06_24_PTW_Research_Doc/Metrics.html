NORTH STAR METRIC: Total permits closed per month with complete digital evidence trail - defined as permits that have (a) all approval levels digitally signed, (b) at least one QR-activated field safety checklist completed, and (c) a closure comment with at least one attachment. This single metric captures depth of platform adoption beyond surface permit creation and directly measures the platform's core value delivery: replacing paper permit governance with a fully traceable digital record.
