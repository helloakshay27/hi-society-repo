TARGET GROUP 3: COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE, DATA CENTERS, AND PREMIUM FACILITY OPERATORS (REITs, Grade-A Offices, Hospitals, Malls)
26
Why This TG - Product Fit Rationale
27
Grade-A commercial buildings, data centers, hospitals, and large malls run high volumes of contractor work in occupied environments where permit failures have catastrophic consequences: tenant safety incidents, patient harm, data center downtime, or mall fire events. These buyers are not Factory Act regulated like manufacturing but face RERA, NBC 2016, NABH, and LEED/IGBC audit obligations. The QR geo-fenced field execution (preventing contractors from activating permits in wrong zones of a live occupied building) and the location-based permit activation feature are the key differentiation points for this segment. REIT operators managing 20M+ sq ft of commercial real estate are single-deal multi-site opportunities.
