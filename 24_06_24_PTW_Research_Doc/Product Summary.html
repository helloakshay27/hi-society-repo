THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
10
Core Pain PointOrganizations managing contractor-heavy or multi-site operations rely on paper permits, WhatsApp approvals, or fragmented spreadsheets to authorize high-risk work. This creates safety gaps where work starts without completed safety validations, approvals from the wrong roles, missing JSAs, unverified vendor qualifications, or no real-time status visibility. Incidents, audit failures, and regulatory fines follow.
11
Data Sovereignty GapWhen permit records sit in paper registers, email chains, or shared drives, there is no single audit-ready trail. Safety officers cannot prove that all approvals happened before work started, that checklists were completed at the worksite, or that the permit was closed only after all mandatory evidence was recorded.
12
Switching Cost TrapCompeting EHS platforms (VelocityEHS, Intelex, Safetymint) bundle PTW inside large EHS suites requiring 6-18 month implementations, complex IT dependencies, and $500-$3,000/month enterprise pricing. FM Matrix PTW delivers focused permit governance with QR field execution, JSA workflows, and configurable templates at a fraction of the total cost and implementation timeline.

FEATURE SUMMARY
19
FM Matrix PTW covers nine functional modules: Permit Request and Creation (auto-capture, structured hazard selection, vendor assignment, multi-activity support); Vendor Completion and Supporting Documents (inbox, prefilled forms, supervisor and manpower capture, document placeholders); Permit Review, Approval and Sign-off (digital signatures, JSA processing, approval routing, level-wise tracking); QR and Field Safety Execution (unique QR post-approval, mobile scanning, geo-fenced activation, real-time checklists, photo/video evidence, negative response auto-hold); Permit Monitoring and List Management (centralized register, multi-status visibility, validity tracking, filters); Permit Control Actions (extension management, hold/resume with undertaking, auto-suspension, formal closure with mandatory evidence); Communication and Collaboration (comment logs, escalation emails, stakeholder notifications); Master Setup and Configuration (permit taxonomy, preloaded templates, JSA templates, RBAC, approval rule engine, auto-calculated validity); and Dashboard, Reporting and Audit (real-time graphical dashboard, site-wise comparison, full audit trail, incident and asset integration).
