THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
11
Core Pain PointCommercial property managers across India's tech parks, SEZs, malls, and mixed-use developments operate on a cocktail of WhatsApp groups, physical notice boards, spreadsheets, and vendor calls. A tech park housing 50 tenants may have 3 separate WhatsApp groups, 2 email threads, and zero visibility on service request resolution times. When a tenant escalates, no one has an audit trail.
12
Data Sovereignty GapEvery major tenant experience platform - HqO, Equiem, Yardi CommercialCafe - stores tenant data on vendor-controlled cloud infrastructure in US or EU data centers. India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 and enterprise data governance mandates from BFSI, pharma, and defense tenants require that sensitive employee activity, visitor logs, and transaction data cannot reside on third-party servers. Lockated is structurally compliant by design.
13
Switching Cost TrapLandlords currently running one-off apps or legacy CAFM tools are locked into vendors who hold their operational data hostage. Lockated's on-premise model means clients own their data from day one, reducing vendor lock-in and providing migration optionality that no SaaS competitor can match.
