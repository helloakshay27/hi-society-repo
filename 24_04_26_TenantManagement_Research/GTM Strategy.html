TG1: Enterprise Landlords and REIT Portfolio Managers
4
Why This Target GroupThese buyers control 80% of Grade A commercial real estate in India. A single deal (Embassy, Mindspace, Prestige) can unlock 10-20 property deployments simultaneously. They have the highest urgency due to DPDP compliance risk with MNC tenants and the competitive pressure to offer digital workplace experiences in lease negotiations.
5
Sales MotionDirect enterprise sales. C-suite approach (COO, CTO, Head of Leasing). Demo focused on DPDP compliance risk documentation, not product features. Proof-of-concept (POC) deployment in one property within 4 weeks. Land one property in a portfolio, expand across portfolio in Year 2. Primary objection: 'We have Yardi / CAFM already' - overcome by showing Yardi has no tenant experience layer and is US-cloud hosted.
6
Marketing ChannelsDirect outreach via PropTech and CRE industry events (FICCI Real Estate Summit, CII Urban Infrastructure, CREDAI events). LinkedIn ABM targeting CRE Operations and Technology heads. Case study content on DPDP compliance in CRE (content marketing). Referrals from existing enterprise tenants (BFSI, pharma) who mandate data residency to their landlords.
7
90-Day Launch SequenceDays 1-30: Identify top 20 target landlord groups from CREDAI and FICCI membership lists. Assign 2 enterprise reps. LinkedIn and warm intro outreach. Target 10 discovery calls. Days 31-60: Deliver 5 live demos. Close 2 POC agreements. POC properties go live with basic modules (OSR, notices, wallet). Days 61-90: POC review with champion. Build case study. Introduce to portfolio expansion conversation. Target 1 signed enterprise deal by Day 90.
8
Partnership StrategyCREDAI and FICCI Real Estate Committee membership for warm introductions. RICS India partnership for credibility with international landlords. IGBC (Indian Green Building Council) partnership for ESG module promotion. Institutional REIT investor relations (Brookfield, Blackstone India) as reference customer intros.
9
TG SUMMARY - TG1 is the highest ACV segment with 3-6 month sales cycles. Key assumption: DPDP compliance urgency is a real buying trigger - this must be validated in the first 20 discovery calls. One metric that tells us it is working: number of POC agreements signed in first 90 days (target: 2).

TG2: FM Companies and Property Management Firms as Channel Partners
12
Why This Target GroupCBRE India, JLL India, Knight Frank, and Cushman and Wakefield collectively manage 1,000+ Grade A commercial properties in India. They are already trusted by the landlords we want to reach. A channel partnership with even one Tier 1 FM firm can deploy Lockated across 50-100 properties with a single commercial agreement. FM firms benefit because they differentiate their service offering and win client mandates by adding technology. This is a distribution advantage no direct sales team can replicate at the same speed.
13
Sales MotionPartnership sales. Target National Technology Heads and Innovation Directors at FM firms. Pitch: white-labeled Lockated as the FM firm's proprietary digital workplace platform. Revenue share model: FM firm retains 25-35% of ARR for properties they bring. FM firm manages client relationship; Lockated provides technology and implementation support. Two-tier deal: (1) Partnership MOU signed, (2) First 3 client deployments as proof of concept.
14
Marketing ChannelsDirect outreach to Innovation and Technology heads at FM firms. Industry conferences: ISHRAE (HVAC/FM), BIFM India, and PropTech India Summit. RICS FM chapter for warm introductions. Industry analyst briefings (JLL Research, CBRE Research) to establish Lockated as the India CRE tenant experience category leader.
15
90-Day Launch SequenceDays 1-30: Map all 40 FM firms managing 50+ commercial properties in India. Identify technology/innovation contact at each. 15 outreach messages. Days 31-60: Deliver 5 partnership presentations to FM firm technology heads. Target 2 MOU discussions. Days 61-90: Sign 1 channel MOU. Identify first 3 client properties from FM firm's portfolio. Begin joint go-to-market with partner.
16
Partnership StrategyCBRE India - target as anchor channel partner for national reach. JLL India - secondary partner for Mumbai and NCR concentration. Local FM firms (Brigade Hospitality Services, Sodexo India) for specific geography penetration. Integration partnerships with CAFM tools (IBM TRIRIGA, Archibus) to fit into existing FM tech stacks.
17
TG SUMMARY - TG2 is the highest leverage segment - one partnership can unlock 50+ deployments. Key assumption: FM firms will white-label rather than build proprietary platforms. This assumption requires validation in first 5 presentations. One metric that tells us it is working: number of FM firm partnership MOU discussions initiated within 60 days.

TG3: Co-working and Flexible Space Operators
20
Why This Target GroupCo-working operators (Awfis, IndiQube, WeWork India, 91springboard, Smartworks) are under intense competition and are differentiating on member experience. They have 10-100 locations, thousands of members, and need booking management, community features, and loyalty programs. Average ACV is lower (INR 30,000-80,000/month per location) but volume is high (800+ co-working locations in India) and sales cycles are shorter (2-6 weeks). Co-working deployments create product-led growth as tenant companies who experience Lockated at co-working spaces become advocates when their parent company evaluates enterprise deployment.
21
Sales MotionProduct-led + direct inside sales. Self-service trial for single-location operators. Inside sales team handles 5-50 location operators via demo and onboarding call. Account management for 50+ location networks. Primary pitch: 'Replace your booking system, WhatsApp group, and loyalty spreadsheet with one platform.' Pricing entry point at INR 30,000/month makes the decision easy for co-working operators used to spending INR 20,000-40,000 on booking software alone.
22
Marketing ChannelsCo-working industry associations (IndiQube events, CoWrks network events). LinkedIn targeting co-working Founders, GMs, and Operations Heads. Product-led growth: free trial for single location. Case study: '40% improvement in meeting room utilization at [co-working brand].' Referral program: co-working operators who refer Grade A office clients receive 3 months free.
23
90-Day Launch SequenceDays 1-30: Build co-working operator database (800+ locations). Launch outreach campaign targeting 50 operators with 5+ locations. Enable free 30-day trial for single-location signups. Days 31-60: 20 demos delivered. 5 trials converted to paid. First 5 success stories documented. Days 61-90: 10 paid deployments. 2 multi-location operators signed. First referral from co-working to Grade A office client generated.
24
Partnership StrategyCo-working industry body IndiQube Network for member introductions. WeWork India as anchor brand reference client. Awfis and Smartworks as Tier 2 anchor brands. Cross-sell to enterprise when co-working members recommend platform to their parent company IT or CRE teams.
25
TG SUMMARY - TG3 is the fastest entry path and creates a reference base for enterprise sales. Key assumption: co-working operators will pay for an all-in-one platform if the price is competitive with their current booking-only tools. One metric that tells us it is working: free trial to paid conversion rate within 30 days (target: 30%).
